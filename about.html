About Andrew Abel

Andrew Abel is a software developer based in Silicon Valley, California, originally from Helsby, Cheshire, England. He shares his life with his wife Angela, and takes great pride in his role as grandfather to five grandchildren: Madison, Melia, Luke, Rocky, and Ranger.

Andrew developed his passion for spoken poetry through his grandfather, Wilfred Harrison, who enjoyed reciting poems. This familial influence shaped his creative direction toward children's literature filled with rhyming narratives, warmth, and humour.

His debut books grew from the simple joy of reading to his grandchildren at bedtime — stories invented on the spot that made them laugh, wonder, and ask for more. Andrew has since published eleven books spanning playful picture books, gentle Christian stories, and heartfelt tributes to childhood.

Andrew also produces free videos to accompany his books, bringing the stories to life for young readers. His works are catalogued in WorldCat for library access, and he maintains an Amazon author profile.

He can be reached at andyabel@gmail.com.

Illustrations are sometimes created collaboratively, and some imagery incorporates AI tools from platforms like Canva.
