A Useless Book

By Andrew Abel  |  Ages 4–8

A laugh-out-loud rhyming picture book that proudly claims to teach nothing at all — yet sneaks in real facts about animals, numbers, and the world. For kids who love silliness, randomness, and mischief. Seuss-style humor with playful rhymes that will have children and adults giggling from cover to cover.
