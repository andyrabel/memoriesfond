Big Bad Wolf's Birthday

Written & Illustrated by Andrew Abel  |  Ages 4–10

A hilarious sequel to The Three Little Pigs — the reformed wolf, now vegan and best friends with the pigs, celebrates his birthday with balloon-blowing, ironic piggy presents, and vegan cupcakes. Clever rhymes, witty twists, and hand-painted illustrations make it a firm bedtime favorite with grandchildren everywhere.
