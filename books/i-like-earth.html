I Like Earth

By Andrew Abel  |  Ages 3–6

A rhyming picture book exploring the solar system — from the Sun and Moon through to Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune, and even Pluto — to find the best planet to live on. It turns out Earth, with its oceans, mountains, and fresh air to breathe, is pretty much perfect. A child's-eye view of why home is best.

Author's Note

"I have long marvelled at how perfectly suited Earth is to life, and how inhospitable the rest of the solar system is. But after hearing a message from a scientist friend on the uniqueness of our planet, I found myself freshly awed. So I thought it would be fun to write a book from a child's perspective — trying to decide which planet to live on. Most books about space are about the excitement of going somewhere new. I thought it would be fun to contrast that with someone who, after careful consideration, would be perfectly happy staying right where they are."
