Seasons with Dad

By Andrew Abel  |  Illustrated by Graeme Holding  |  Ages 5–12

A heartwarming picture book celebrating family bonding and nature across the seasons. Lyrical verses and vibrant illustrations depict spring wildflowers, summer steam trains, autumn leaves, and winter sledging — inspired by real locations in Northwest England and North Wales. The book Andrew says means the most to him personally.

About the Illustrator

Graeme Holding is originally from Helsby, Cheshire, and now lives in Runcorn with his wife and four children. His illustration style is bright, fun, traditional but quirky. He completed a Children's Book Illustration course at the London Art College. Portfolio: graemeholdingillustration.com
