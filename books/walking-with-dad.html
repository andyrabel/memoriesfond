Walking with Dad

By Andrew Abel  |  Illustrated by Graeme Holding  |  Ages 3–7

A poetic celebration of the precious bond between a child and their father — built not on material things or grand conversations, but on shared, everyday moments. Written in lyrical rhyming verse with a glossary of vocabulary words, this hardcover picture book has become the bestselling title in my collection and a favorite Father's Day gift.

About the Illustrator

Graeme Holding is originally from Helsby, Cheshire, and now lives in Runcorn with his wife and four children. His illustration style is bright, fun, traditional but quirky. He completed a Children's Book Illustration course at the London Art College. Portfolio: graemeholdingillustration.com
