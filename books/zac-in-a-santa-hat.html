Zac in a Santa Hat

By Andrew Abel  |  Ages 4–8  |  Published 2026

Zac returns with caroling friends to share a Christmas song about a baby born in a manger. A neighbour joins them in learning the tune and shares refreshments. Playful rhymes, warm illustrations, and a traditional holiday carol invite families to sing along while exploring Christmas's deeper meaning.

"Sweet… encourages families to reflect on the true significance of Christmas." — Crystal Bowman, bestselling award-winning children's author (100+ books)
